Our Mission

SAFER exists to ensure no one meets crisis alone. We place women at the heart of our mission — because they are the primary victims of gender‑based violence — but our protocol is built for anyone facing a life‑threatening situation. SAFER is powered by an active community across the country: when you trigger an alert, your 3 trusted contacts and nearby SAFER users are notified instantly. If no one responds, the alert escalates automatically. You are never left without a lifeline.
